A Message from Stink Floyd!

The Story of Stink

Nicholas "Stink Floyd" Tzavaras was born on Christmas Day in 1985. It is rumored he was not born from a womb, but from a brick pizza oven. Unstoppable in the kitchen and on the virtual fields of battle, Nicholas wanted to combine his passions for the world. It was with this vision that he created Stinkfloyd's Pizza Arcade.

Stinkfloyd's Pizza Arcade was opened in 2018 with one goal; Bring the fun! At Stinkfloyd's, we like to think of the whole picture. We started by building lasting partnerships with local sources for ingredients. These partnerships not only deliver our customers the freshest and most savory food, but also promotes a healthy local economy. By supporting our neighbors, we support not only ourselves, but the entire community. That's thinking outside the pizza box!

Continuing the trend of completeness, why just stop at delicious pizza? We have over 40 beers, taps or bottles, with local to imported varieties. These beers are always rotating! Ask a server when you come in for an up-to-date listing. Want a bottle? We have all the classics in an a cold glass bottle just for you.

After putting together a list of sources and beer, We had to stop and think. We felt we were missing something. We wanted Stinkfloyd's to feel like the weekend everyday. Nothing says weekend like a little video game competion with your friends, so we brought them in! Our arcade is HUGE! We have all the classics, from Pacman and Paperboy, to the Simpsons and Ninja Turtles. Stinkfloyd's knows that fun knows no generation, thats why we also have TEN booths equipped with giant 4k TV's with EVERY gaming system rentable. Play NES Super Mario, or smash your friends on Nintendo's new Switch system. Sony, Microsoft and Nintendo systems are all rentable.

We are available for catering off site, as well as large parties on site. Our staff is full service, friendly, and fun. This allows you to not worry about serving your event, or keeping them happy, allowing you to also enjoy yourself. Our entire store or a table can be reserved for a party, and all prices include service.
